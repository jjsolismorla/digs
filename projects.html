Projects

These projects were creating because I had no other choice. I couldn't help but to dig deeper into my own history, my family's and my country's. From fiction aimed to illustrate some of the experiences of a gay man growing up in a homophobic environment to academic explorations of Ecuadorian history, I hope these projects inspire and accompany you in your own explorations. In that process, you will understand more about the elements that make you you and discover aspects of your personality and history that can hopefully help others in their process.
